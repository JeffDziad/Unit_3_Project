Welcome, To Camp Flambeau!

        At Camp Flambeau, we pride ourselves on giving our customers the ultimate northern Wisconsin experience. From the crystal clear waters of Fence Lake and its chain lakes, all the way to the quaint and lively city of Minocqua, just fifteen minutes away. The area surrounding Camp Flambeau is filled with activites and enjoyable adventures waiting to be discovered. We are sure you'll find your stay enjoyable.

Activities Available!

        Camp Flambeau features two Cabins overlooking Fence Lake with a beautiful western exposure and a feeling of being secluded from urban life. Should you rent a boat or bring your own, Fence lake provides over 3,400 acres of open waters for water skiing, tubing, swimming, or just taking a sunset cruise. If you happen to explore all of Fence lake, its chain lakes provide another 4,400 acres of open waters waiting to be enjoyed. The town of Minocqua, also known as "The Island City", brings the best of urban lifestyle to the wooded forests of norther Wisconsin. Head to their village district to shop or visit one of their many Restaurants that overlook the surrounding Minocqua Lake. The possibilities are endless when visiting Camp Flambeau.
